No Refund Policy

All sales of digital products are final. Because our programs, guides and trackers are delivered digitally and can be accessed immediately after purchase, we do not offer refunds, returns or exchanges for digital products.

  • No refunds are given for changing your mind after purchase.
  • No refunds are given if you buy the wrong program.
  • No refunds are given if you do not use the program.
  • No refunds are given for digital products once access has been provided.
Important

Customers should read the product page carefully before buying, especially for digital products with instant access.

Physical Product Policy

Recommended products and physical products may be fulfilled manually or through third-party suppliers. Delivery times, availability and product packaging may vary depending on the supplier.

  • FitBrand is not responsible for delays caused by wrong customer information.
  • If a product arrives damaged, the customer must contact us with clear photos and order information.

Customer Responsibility

Fitness results depend on effort, consistency, nutrition, training history and personal lifestyle. FitBrand does not guarantee specific results. The customer is responsible for using the programs safely and appropriately.

  • Always train with proper form and safety.
  • Stop training if you feel pain, dizziness or unusual discomfort.
  • Consult a professional if you have health concerns before starting a program.

Digital products are for personal use only. The customer may not resell, share, upload, copy or distribute FitBrand programs, guides or trackers without written permission.

  • Digital access is intended only for the customer who purchased it.

Payment Policy

Orders are only confirmed after successful payment. If payment fails, access or fulfillment will not be provided. FitBrand may update prices at any time without notice.

Privacy Policy
