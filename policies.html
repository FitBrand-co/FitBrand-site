FitBrand content is general fitness and nutrition guidance. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, treatment or a replacement for advice from a doctor, dietitian, physiotherapist or other qualified professional.

  • Train safely, use proper technique and stop if you feel pain, dizziness, chest pain or unusual discomfort.

  • Meal plans, calories and macro estimates are general estimates and may not be appropriate for every person.
  • FitBrand does not guarantee specific fat loss, muscle gain, strength gain, health outcomes or body transformation results.
