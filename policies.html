FitBrand Policies

Policies, privacy and safety.

These policies explain how FitBrand works during our early-access validation phase. No payment is currently taken through the website.

TermsEarly accessAge rulesHealth disclaimerAI toolsPrivacyCookiesFuture paymentsSupport

1. Terms of use

FitBrand is a fitness, content and community platform currently testing interest in digital training programs, meal planning tools and related products. By using the website, joining early access or creating an account, you agree to use the site responsibly and not misuse, copy, resell or redistribute FitBrand content.

2. Early access / waitlist

Early access is not a purchase. It is a demand-validation list that helps us understand which products people want before payments open. Joining the waitlist does not guarantee access, pricing, discounts or availability. We may contact you about launch updates, beta testing and feedback.

3. Age rules

FitBrand is for users aged 13+. Users aged 13–17 must have permission from a parent or legal guardian before using FitBrand, joining early access, creating an account or using AI tools. We may refuse access if age or permission is unclear.

4. Health and fitness disclaimer

FitBrand content is for general fitness information only and is not medical advice. Training and nutrition changes can involve risk. Users should stop if they feel pain, dizziness or unusual symptoms and should speak with a qualified professional before starting if they have medical conditions, injuries or concerns.

5. AI tools and generated plans

FitBrand AI tools may generate training or meal suggestions based on user inputs. AI output can be imperfect and should be reviewed with common sense. Users are responsible for checking food allergies, medical needs, exercise technique and suitability.

6. Privacy policy

We may collect information such as name, email, waitlist choices, product interest, goals, account status, device/browser information and messages sent to support. We use this to provide accounts, waitlist updates, support, website functionality, analytics and product improvement. We do not sell personal information.

You can contact us to request correction or deletion of personal data where applicable.

7. Cookies and local storage

FitBrand uses necessary browser storage for login, profile state, cart, waitlist form behaviour and website functionality. We may use analytics tools such as Vercel Speed Insights to understand site performance. Blocking necessary storage may affect functionality.

8. Future payments, subscriptions and refunds

Payments are not currently active. If FitBrand later opens paid products or subscriptions, prices, billing frequency, cancellation rules, refund rules and digital access terms will be shown clearly before checkout. We will update these policies before accepting real payments.

9. Support and contact

For questions, account help, early access, privacy requests or complaints, contact: support@fitbrand.fit.

These policies are templates and should be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before FitBrand begins taking real customer payments.
